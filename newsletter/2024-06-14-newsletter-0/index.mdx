## GRC: Advancing the Future of High-Performance Computing Storage Technologies
We are thrilled to announce the launch of the Gnosis Research Center (GRC) at the Illinois Institute of Technology (IIT). The GRC is dedicated to advancing the frontiers of Data Management in High-Performance Computing (HPC) by designing and developing scalable software solutions for memory, storage, and I/O systems. 



## Featured GRC Projects
1. [Hermes: Extending the HDF Library to Support Intelligent I/O Buffering for Deep Memory and Storage Hierarchy System](https://grc.iit.edu/research/projects/hermes)
2. [ChronoLog: A High-Performance Storage Infrastructure for Activity and Log Workloads](https://grc.iit.edu/research/projects/chronolog)
3. [Coeus: Accelerating Scientific Insights Using Enriched Metadata](https://grc.iit.edu/research/projects/coeus)
